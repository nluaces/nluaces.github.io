whoami

Hello, I'm Noe Luaces and I'm a software engineer, among other things. The purpose of this personal website is to share my experience and my personal/parallel projects that I've been working on through my career. In 2007, I finished my 3 year Bachelor Degree in Computer Science but I always wanted to learn more of subjects that weren't included in my degree, so I enrolled a course to adapt my old degree to 4 year Bachelor Degree while working full time (don't recommend doing that anymore though). In that course I've learned about Software Architecture models and techniques, and also I was exposed to functional programming. I've also worked on initiatives to keep women developers in the industry offering them support that I know that is needed by experience. The reason of that is the hostility that remains in some environments towards women -or diverse people in general-

I'm not consider myself an expert in other areas, I will say that I keep insisting on practicing some hobbies through the years, like drawing, taking pictures with plastic cameras with plastic lenses (like the one in the photo) or with my phone, read and accumulate comics (Japanese, Franco-Belgian or American indie comics), over-analising indie movies and sometimes criticising the mainstream ones. All of these things influence, some how, the rest of my work. I don't work as an illustrator, but I usually create my own drawings to use them in the posts of my blogs. Another scenario is the one where I use the name "Elaine Marley" to mock a data class in a test, and I see that someone added a new test case with "Bob Marley". In that case, I have no choice but suggest that person to play Monkey Island, but only the first two parts.
